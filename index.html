Privacy Policy

Last updated: 25 May 2026  ·  App: Caltrak

Caltrak is a personal calorie and nutrition tracking app developed by Blaz Apps. This policy explains what data the app collects, how it is used, and your rights regarding that data.

1. Data We Collect

Caltrak collects only the information you enter directly into the app:

  • Food and meal logs (name, portion, calories, macronutrients)
  • Daily nutrition goals and preferences you configure
  • Any notes or custom entries you create

We do not collect your name, email address, location, device identifiers, or any other personal information.

2. How Your Data Is Stored

All data is stored locally on your device only. Caltrak does not operate any servers and does not transmit your data to us or any third party automatically.

3. Third-Party App Integration

Caltrak may allow you to connect to third-party health or fitness platforms (for example, Google Fit or similar services) to sync your nutrition data. This integration is entirely optional and initiated by you. When you choose to sync:

  • Data is sent directly to the third-party service under their own privacy policy.
  • Blaz Apps does not receive, store, or have access to data shared with those services.
  • You can disconnect the integration at any time from within the app or your device settings.

4. Crash Reporting & Analytics

Caltrak does not currently use any crash reporting or analytics services. If this changes in a future update, this policy will be updated before the release, and the policy will clearly describe what is collected and by whom.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any third party for advertising, marketing, or any other commercial purpose.

6. Data Deletion

Because all data is stored on your device, you can delete it at any time by clearing the app's data from your device settings or by uninstalling the app. No data remains on any server after uninstallation.

7. Children's Privacy

Caltrak is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. If you believe a child has entered personal data into the app, you can remove it by clearing the app's data on their device.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, the "Last updated" date at the top of this page will change. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
